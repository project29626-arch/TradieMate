# Business Context

## Company Name
TradieMate

## Product or Service
TradieMate is an AI-powered, voice-first admin platform built as a mobile-friendly web app (PWA) for Australian tradie businesses. Core capabilities:

- **AI voice quoting** (lead wedge): Tradie speaks a quote on-site; AI formats and sends it to the client instantly — no typing required.
- **Voice site reports + photo**: Dictate a report on-site with accompanying photos; AI generates a clean written report.
- **AI client follow-up**: Automated follow-up on pending quotes, approvals, and outstanding invoices.
- **AI missed-call handling**: AI phones the customer back when the tradie can't answer, books the job or takes a message.
- **Task management**: Simple on-phone task and job tracking with AI assistance.

The product is delivered as a PWA (mobile-friendly web app) — no App Store required, lives on the home screen, works in the field.

## ICP
Australian tradie business owners and sole traders across all trade verticals (plumbers, electricians, builders, concreters, carpenters, tilers, etc.) who:
- Run 1–5 person operations
- Are time-poor and admin-averse — they'd rather be on the tools
- Miss calls and lose jobs because they're on-site and can't answer the phone
- Currently quote by memory, text message, or rough paper — no formal system
- Are smartphone-native but not software-native

**Note:** Targeting all trade verticals from day one is a deliberate choice. The risk is that early messaging will be generic. Mitigation: use personal network for the first cohort, which will likely cluster naturally around one or two verticals — let that shape the reference story.

## Mission
To give every Australian tradie their time back by replacing phone-tag, paperwork, and missed calls with AI that works as hard as they do.

## Business Classification
- Company Type: B2B SaaS startup
- Industry: Construction Tech / Field Services Tech (TradieTech)
- User Type: SMB / Sole trader

## Company Values
- Field-first: every feature is designed to work with one hand, on a roof, in a crawl space
- Straight talk: no jargon, no fluff — tradies call it like it is and so do we
- Time is the product: if a feature doesn't save minutes, it doesn't ship
- Community before scale: grow through trust and referrals, not spray-and-pray ads
- Built in Australia: designed around Australian trade workflows, licensing, and customer expectations

## Go-to-Market Strategy
**Phase 1 — First 10 customers (personal network):**
Direct outreach through Khiem's personal network. First cohort will likely cluster around 1–2 trade verticals naturally — use that to build an early reference story.

**Phase 2 — Community expansion:**
Australian tradie Facebook groups (e.g., "Tradie Hub Australia", state-based groups) are active and high-trust. Peer referrals and case studies from Phase 1 customers drive organic word-of-mouth.

**Phase 3 — TBD:**
Potential supplier partnerships (Reece, Tradelink, Bunnings) or paid digital ads once product-market fit is confirmed.

## Business Model
Monthly SaaS subscription. Pricing TBD — needs validation with early customers.

**Market reference:** Comparable Australian tradie SaaS tools (ServiceM8, Tradify, AroFlo) charge approximately AUD $29–$99/month per user. TradieMate's AI differentiation and voice-first positioning may support pricing at the upper end of that range, but this should be tested, not assumed.

**Open question:** Whether to offer a free trial period (recommended for tradie audience who are skeptical of software until they see it work).

## Unfair Advantage
Early community and word-of-mouth within tradie networks. Tradies trust other tradies — a strong reference customer in a local market can unlock a cluster of referrals quickly.

**Note:** Community is a real early advantage but is not a long-term moat. The AI missed-call feature has stronger technical defensibility as the product matures — worth investing in as a secondary moat.

## One Sentence Summary
TradieMate is an AI-powered, voice-first admin platform that helps Australian tradies send quotes, write site reports, follow up with clients, and never miss a job — all from their phone, without typing.
